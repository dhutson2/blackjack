Fun fact: Blackjack is the casino card game that gives the player the highest odds of winning each hand

Instructions:

The Objective of Blackjack is to get as close as possible to 21 without going over.
If the dealer gets closer to 21 than the player, the dealer will win.

Turns:

Each turn consists of the option to either hit or stand.

- Hit will add another card to hand, which will add to hand point total.
- Stand will take no more turns and keep the current hand point value.

The player can take as many turns as desired, one at a time, until 21 is reached, player stands, or player busts(goes over 21)

How to win:

When the player ends turn (if cards are below 21), the dealer will take a turn until at least 17 is reached or 21 is exceeded.
If a player hits 21, they will win. Once turns are over, whoever has higher card between dealer and player wins.
If both players finish taking turns and hand points are equal, it is a draw
If player wins hand, they get a point. Otherwise, they will lose a point.
